Hulu Hits Back

While Netflix has the most shows for those over 18, Hulu has a clear advantage for younger viewers. Prime Video lags behind in third for most categories, with Disney+ a distant fourth.

Prime Time

Prime Video makes a compelling case for nostalgic viewers with the most options for shows from before the 2000s. Meanwhile, Hulu and Netflix offer a plethora of modern options with Prime not far behind. Disney+ continues to disappoint with less than stellar numbers. As to be expected with such a new service.

Disney+ Seizing the Moment

While Netflix has a hefty headstart on its competitors in terms of numbers of movies on the left, Amazon Prime holds a solid second, as at 5590 to 2775 movies respectively, Netflix has more movies in its possession than all its competitors combined. In addition to this, the average rating (as shown on the right) of a Netflix movie is consistently among the largest, regardless of the decade of the movie's origin. However, the rating of Amazon Prime movies is consistently the lowest of all services, making Netflix the clear frontrunner. Although Disney+ is a newcomer to streaming services, it also is ranked highly in terms of average Movie rating, and of the most recent movies made it surpasses even Netflix. Backed by the exclusivity they have created, it seems likely that Disney may have found a solid foothold though which to expand further into this business.

Time to Crown the Winner

After going through the analysis, despite the valiant efforts demonstrated by Prime Video and Hulu, it appears that Netflix is the clearest winner. With an impressive amount of highly rated shows, an excellent repertoire of shows for each age group, and competitive numbers for older and newer shows, Netflix demonstrates the best all around statistics for the different categories we looked at. While we have deemed Netflix the winner, Hulu and Amazon deserve an honorable mention for their mostly competitive numbers. Lastly, Disney+ has disappointed us with it's poor performance, but we are hopeful for a strong comeback in the future. Happy binging!
