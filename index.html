To View or Not to View

Which Service Offers the Best Content?

Presented by: Bailey Man and Nigel Doering


Netflix Strikes First

Netflix demonstrates a clear advantage with the number of highly rated shows

Hulu Hits Back

While Netflix has the most shows for those over 18, Hulu has a clear advantage for younger viewers. Prime Video lags behind in third for most categories, with Disney+ a distant fourth.

Prime Time

Prime Video makes a compelling case for nostalgic viewers with the most options for movies from before the 2000s. Meanwhile, Hulu and Netflix offer a plethora of modern options with Prime not far behind. Disney+ continues to disappoint with less than stellar numbers. As to be expected with such a new service.

Time to Crown the Winner

After going through the analysis, despite the valiant efforts demonstrated by Prime Video and Hulu, it appears that Netflix is the clearest winner. With an impressive amount of highly rated shows, an excellent repertoire of shows for each age group, and competitive numbers for older and newer shows, Netflix demonstrates the best all around statistics for the different categories we looked at. While we have deemed Netflix the winner, Hulu and Amazon deserve an honorable mention for their mostly competitive numbers. Lastly, Disney+ has disappointed us with it's poor performance, but we are hopeful for a strong comeback in the future. Happy binging!
